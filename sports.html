The Legacy of Maharashtra Sports:A Tour of the State's Iconic Stadiums


"Maharashtra is home to several world-class sports stadiums that have hosted numerous international and domestic sporting events. The state is especially renowned for its cricket stadiums, with Wankhede Stadium in Mumbai and VCA Stadium in Nagpur being two of the most iconic cricket grounds in India. The state also has multi-purpose stadiums like DY Patil Stadium in Navi Mumbai and Balewadi Stadium in Pune that host cricket, football, and other sporting events. Apart from hosting sporting events, these stadiums also offer guided tours to visitors who can get a behind-the-scenes look at the facilities and learn about the history and heritage of these iconic venues. With state-of-the-art technology, world-class amenities, and a rich sporting legacy, the stadiums in Maharashtra are a testament to the state's passion for sports and its commitment to promoting a sporting culture."

01. Wankhede Stadium

Wankhede Stadium is a cricket stadium located in the southern part of Mumbai, Maharashtra. It was established in 1974 and has a seating capacity of approximately 33,000 spectators. The stadium is owned by the Mumbai Cricket Association (MCA) and has hosted numerous... international cricket matches, including the 2011 ICC Cricket World Cup Final, which India won.
The playing area was also redesigned to meet international standards. Wankhede Stadium is known for its lively atmosphere, especially during Indian Premier League (IPL) matches when it is packed with passionate cricket fans.

02. D Y Patil sports stadium

DY Patil Stadium is a multi-purpose stadium located in Navi Mumbai, Maharashtra. It was established in 2008 and has a seating capacity of approximately 55,000 spectators. The stadium is owned by the DY Patil Sports Academy and has hosted numerous cricket, football..., and other sporting events.The stadium is known for its state-of-the-art facilities and world-class amenities. It has a FIFA-standard football ground, an indoor cricket academy, and a swimming pool. It has hosted several Indian Premier League (IPL) matches and international cricket matches, including the 2008 Indian Premier League Final and the 2010 Commonwealth Games boxing events.

03. Ramsheth Thakur International Sports Complex

Ramsheth Thakur International Sports Complex is a multi-sport stadium located in Navi Mumbai, Maharashtra. It was established in 2014 and has a seating capacity of approximately 10,000 spectators. The stadium is owned by the City and Industrial Development Corporation (CIDCO)... and has hosted numerous cricket, football, and other sporting events.
The stadium is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities including a floodlit cricket ground, football ground, an Olympic size swimming pool, a badminton hall, a basketball court, a squash court, and a gymnasium. The complex also has a sports academy that provides training and coaching in various sports.

04. Mumbai Football Arena

Mumbai Football Arena is a multi-purpose stadium located in Andheri, Mumbai. It was established in 2016 and has a seating capacity of approximately 8,000 spectators. The stadium is owned by the Mumbai City FC and has hosted numerous football matches.... Apart from football matches, Mumbai Football Arena has also hosted music concerts and other cultural events. The stadium offers guided tours to visitors who can get a behind-the-scenes look at the facilities and learn about the history and heritage of the stadium.Overall, Mumbai Football Arena is an important sports venue in Mumbai, playing a significant role in promoting football and providing world-class facilities to athletes and sports enthusiasts.

05. Balewadi Stadium

Balewadi Stadium is a multi-purpose stadium located in Balewadi, Pune. It was established in 1994 and has a seating capacity of approximately 12,000 spectators. The stadium is owned by the Maharashtra government and has hosted numerous sporting events....The stadium is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities including a synthetic track for athletics, a natural grass football field, a swimming pool, a gymnasium, and an indoor stadium for various sports. It has hosted several national and international sporting events, including the Commonwealth Youth Games and the Pune International Marathon.Apart from sporting events, Balewadi Stadium has also hosted music concerts and other cultural events. The stadium offers guided tours to visitors who can get a behind-the-scenes look at the facilities and learn about the history and heritage of the stadium.Overall, Balewadi Stadium is an important sports venue in Pune, playing a significant role in promoting sports and providing world-class facilities to athletes and sports enthusiasts.

06. Mahindra Hockey Stadium

Mahindra Hockey Stadium is a field hockey stadium located in Churchgate, Mumbai. The stadium was established in 2008 and has a seating capacity of approximately 5,000 spectators. It is owned by the Mahindra Group and is primarily used for field hockey matches and training....The stadium is equipped with a state-of-the-art artificial turf pitch, floodlights, and modern facilities for players, coaches, and officials. It has hosted several national and international field hockey matches and tournaments, including the Hockey India League and the Hockey World Cup.Mahindra Hockey Stadium is an important sports venue in Mumbai, playing and providing world-class facilities to athletes and sports enthusiasts.

07. NSCI Dome - Pro Kabaddi

The NSCI Dome is a multi-purpose indoor stadium located in Worli, Mumbai. It was inaugurated in 2017 and has a seating capacity of approximately 8,000 spectators. The stadium is primarily used for indoor sports and events, including concerts and exhibitions....The NSCI Dome has hosted several Pro Kabaddi League (PKL) matches since its inception. It is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities, including a world-class indoor playing surface, high-quality sound and lighting systems,comfortable seating arrangements.
Overall, the NSCI Dome is an important sports and entertainment venue in Mumbai, offering world-class facilities to athletes and sports enthusiasts.

08. MCA Cricket Stadium

The MCA (Maharashtra Cricket Association) Stadium is a cricket stadium located in Gahunje, Pune. It was established in 2012 and has a seating capacity of approximately 37,000 spectators. The stadium is owned by the Maharashtra Cricket Association and is primarily used for hosting domestic... and international cricket matches.The MCA Stadium is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities, including a world-class playing surface, floodlights, and modern amenities for players, coaches, and officials. It has hosted several high-profile cricket matches, including IPL (Indian Premier League) matches, and is the home ground of the Pune Warriors India and the Rising Pune Supergiant teams.
