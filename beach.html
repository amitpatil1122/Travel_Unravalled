Best Beaches in Maharashtra


"Maharashtra's coastline boasts of some of the most beautiful beaches in India. The golden sand beaches, clear blue waters, and serene coastline create a tranquil and picturesque setting for all kinds of beach-goers. From the vibrant nightlife and water sports of Goa's beaches to the secluded coves of the Konkan coastline, Maharashtra's beaches offer a diverse range of experiences. Dip your toes in the warm Arabian Sea, indulge in a relaxing massage, take a stroll along the coastline or simply laze on the beach and soak up the sun. The beaches of Maharashtra are a perfect escape for those seeking a serene, peaceful, and unforgettable experience."

01. Varsoli Beach

Varsoli beach is one of the least visited beaches of Alibaug. It isn't visited by many tourists and hence remains less crowded than other beaches of Alibaug. Located just 3 km away from the city, Varsoli beach is known for its beach resorts and cottages that are best suited for a peaceful getaway. ... The beach serves as a naval base for Indian army and is also known for its variety of adventure sports.

Varsoli is a beautiful white-sand beach coupled with the calm waters of the Arabian sea. The coastline of the Varsoli beach is 2 km long laden with the numerous palm, suru and casuarina trees.

02. Kashid Beach

Located almost 30 km from Alibaug and 125 km from Mumbai, Kashid Beach is situated on the coasts of Arabian Sea. Famous for its beautiful white colour sand, Kashid is one of the cleanest beaches in north Konkan. The beach is stretched across 3 km and surrounded by shrubs and trees called Casuarina....

One can indulge in watersports, go night camping and enjoy the beautiful night sky or just go for horseriding on the beach. Visit Murud Janjira Fort located at a distance of 20 km from Kashid for a slice of history. Weekends at Kashid beach are quite crowded so it would be best to visit on weekdays.

03. Harihareshwar Beach

One of the less crowded beaches in Maharashtra, the Harihareshwar beach is located in the Raigad district, around 200km from Mumbai. Surrounded by four hills named Harihareshwar, Harshinachal, Bramhadri and Pushpadri, Harihareshwar is often referred to as Dev-Ghar... or "house of God".

It is usually called as 'Dakshin Kashi' because of its temple dedicated to Lord Shiva. The place has a lot of accommodation options according to every budget and liking.Harihareshwar has two beaches on either side of Harihareshwar temple, the main attraction of Harihareshwar.

04. Juhu Beach

Smacked right near the city center, Juhu Beach is synonymous to Mumbai. The vast expanse of water stretching across the Arabian Sea glistening under the crimson sun and crashing in a starry night is what makes up for one of the most surreal beaches in Maharashtra.Get your kids to run around ...the shore in their flip flops or maybe lie under the sun soaking in the warmth and getting that much-wanted while you sip in bottles of beer.
You can even go on for an unforgettable horse ride along the shore. And if that wasn’t enough to make your visit an enthralling one, the vendors serving finger-licking-good street food do the job.

06. Kondivli Beach

Maharashtra Beaches adorn the Konkan coast, Kondivli Beach being one of them. Get yourself ready to drink in the thrill of paragliding and other water sports at Kondivli Beach. Moreover, your inner adventure junkie would be delighted by the prospect of trekking along the very robust cliffs of Shrivardhan....

Imagine witnessing a blend of adventure and nature to rejuvenate yourself during the weekend. Sounds intriguing, eh? Get behind the wheels and head to Shrivardhan already!

07. Revdanda Beach

What if history amalgamates with nature? Get yourself to feel like a character in a Bollywood movie at Revdanda Beach. One of the most fascinating beaches in Maharashtra, Revdanda Beach is one of a kind. The Portuguese fort in its ruined state is surrounded by a stretch of a sea....

Look into the cannon holed on the walls of this rustic fort and admire the exotic beauty of the surrounded embracing the same. Revdanda Beach is a must visit for anyone who happens to enter the spectacular territory of Maharashtra.

08. Mandawa Beach

The pristine beach of Mandwa among the endless beaches in Maharashtra is so much more than a weekend retreat. The water sports along with a view so spectacular that it might take your breath away welcome you with a warm embrace at the Mandwa Beach. Did you know that you can... even take a fun ferry ride to this aqua wonder of a place from Mumbai? Well, now you do! Walk on the sand and let loose in the waves crashing your feet.

09. Velas Beach

Up for an offbeat exploration of Maharashtra Beaches? How about witnessing the spectacular Olive Ridley Turtle Hatchlings coming out of the beach to bask in the fresh breeze along the shore. Get away on a weekend to this sojourn place and experience the power and beauty of nature... and beaches to refresh and offer an enthralling experience.

Book a homestay on the beach and make memories while you sit under a starry sky and marvel at the beauty of waves crashing and calming all at once.

10. Guhaghar Beach

Indulge in the sunset and the sunrise at one of the most breathtakingly beautiful beaches in Maharashtra. Guhagar Beach takes pride in being the first white sand beach along the pristine Konkan coast. Situated along the temple town of Guhagar, Guhagar beach is a refreshing and spiritually... sound retreat for a weekend. Take a leap into the water and soak in the under the sun leaving all the problems behind.

11. Velneshwar Beach

Venture into the territory of a rock- less beach in the list of some tranquil beaches in Maharashtra. Velneshwar Beach is just the beach that you need to go to find yourself and the peace within.The sparkling clean beach offers a spectacular view of refreshing beetle but trees in abundance.... Furthermore, the Velneshwar temple adds up to the charm and spirituality of the beach. Lace around in the serenity of the beach and thank the gods for a moment of peace.

12. Kihim Beach

Steer clear of pollution on your next weekend a rejuvenate yourself at one of the most pristine Beaches that Maharashtra is proud of. Kihim Beach offers a clean and refreshing weekend escape to you. Lie down under the sun and get that tan that everyone drools looking at. Also, being one of the... beaches in Alibaug, Kihim beach is popular among travelers. Care to explore this one for yourself?

13. Nagaon Beach

The Nagaon Beach of Alibaug is popular for picnic getaways and its super clear water. It is a top-notch beauty of the state that is a staple on any Maharashtra tour. With the perks of a boat ride, kayaking and island trips, a day spent on the shore of the crashing Arabian Sea here feels no less than... a jarring movie scene.
It sets your spirits free and oozes out positivity from every nook and cranny. No wonder people love spending hours relaxing and collecting betel nuts and coconuts just for the sake of it here.

14. Akshi Beach

Minimalistic yet tranquil, in a nutshell, this pretty much defines Akshi Beach. Here you can live and breathe in fisherman culture of Maharashtra while sun basking on palm laden sands. Watch yourself go completely amazeballs in the privacy of your loved ones. Enjoy the sights of beautiful migratory birds... and if that’s still not enough to please your travel appetite, it only just to compensate for it by gorging on some authentic Maharashtrian cuisine. Sounds like a plan to us, what say?

15. Chivla Beach

The blend of tranquility and adventure sports is always lethal, and this one is too but in a good way. Not a few kilometers from Malvan is this beautiful beach that’s famous for its blissful ambiance.Don’t worry it is very much capable of getting your adrenaline alive and kicking with diving, snorkeling etc.... Not into adventure sports? Worry not! The beach has some bird watching experiences and beautiful monuments lined up for you.
