Maharashtra Hotels & Places to Stay

Maharashtra is a state located in the western region of India and is home to a wide range of hotels, resorts, and accommodations to suit every type of traveler. From luxurious hotels and heritage properties to budget-friendly guesthouses and homestays, Maharashtra has a lot to offer. In Mumbai, the state's bustling capital city, you can find top-rated hotels that cater to both business and leisure travelers. Many of these hotels are located in prime locations, offering easy access to the city's famous landmarks, shopping centers, and restaurants. If you're looking for a beach vacation, Maharashtra has a long coastline and several seaside resorts where you can relax and soak up the sun. Some of the popular beach destinations in Maharashtra include Alibaug, Kashid, and Ganpatipule. For travelers who want to experience the state's rich cultural heritage, Maharashtra has several heritage hotels and homestays that offer a glimpse into the state's history and architecture. These properties are often located in restored forts, palaces, and traditional houses. If you're traveling with your family or pets, there are several family-friendly and pet-friendly hotels in Maharashtra that cater to your needs. Additionally, there are spa resorts and nature resorts in Maharashtra that offer a relaxing retreat amidst scenic natural surroundings.
